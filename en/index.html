Fix it Rent it Print it Get in touch

I like fixing things. Over the years I've gathered a good pile of tools and picked up the skills to use them, so when something breaks my first thought is to repair it rather than throw it out. If you've got something broken, you're missing the right tool, or you just want a small thing 3D printed, you're in the right place. I'm not doing this for a living — there isn't much money in fixing things when most people just buy new — so a small contribution toward materials and the odd new tool is always welcome, but never expected.

🔧

Fix it

Send me your broken stuff and I'll see what I can do. With the right tools and a bit of patience, plenty of things turn out to be an easy fix. And if something already works but could work better with a small tweak or hack, I'm happy to tinker with that too.

🪚

Rent it

I've got plenty of tools sitting around, so why not share them? Friends, family, and people I haven't met yet are all welcome to borrow. Tell me what you need and we'll sort out a reasonable price — a proper list is on the way.

🖨️

Print it coming soon

I don't have a printer yet, but I'd like to. Owning a 3D printer is a whole hobby of its own (and not a cheap one), and commercial printing adds up fast for a single small part. Once I'm set up, I'd like to print little things for people at cost, so getting one small thing printed is finally easy.
